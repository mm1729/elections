Want to know what your party has to do to win the presidency?

Type in results from recent/future house and senate elections to see which state your party is predicted to get in the next presidential election!

Explanation: The scores on the right columns are confidence values, which are between 1 and -1, 1 being you will definitely win and -1 meaning you will lose. The first column shows confidence values for house input and the second column shows confidence values for the senate input. On the bottom is how much your party will most likely have to spend per state for the presidential election, predicted using the house and senate data respecitvely.
NOTE: Leaving out state results, results in the use of default values.
